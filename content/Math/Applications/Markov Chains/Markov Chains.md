Markov chains can model a very wide range of stochastic phenomena. Most of my knowledge of Markov chains come from two books: [Essentials of Stochastic Processes by Rick Durrett](https://sites.math.duke.edu/~rtd/EOSP/EOSP2E.pdf) and [Foundations of Data Science by Blum, Hopcroft, and Kannan](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/jeh/book.pdf).

Most of my study on Markov chains focused on [[Discrete Time Markov Chains]].
