### A Math Major's Apology
- Toward the end of his mathematics career, Tom Hardy wrote a book called *A Mathematician's Apology*. In it, Hardy defended the pursuit of mathematics for the sake of pursuing mathematics. 
	- Hardy argues for the immortality of mathematics. He argues that mathematics has a uniquely beautiful quality of permanence.
	- Almost everything that one creates in their lifetime fades with time. Mathematics on the other hand, is forever a truth of the universe.
