Math sometimes gets a bad rep for being useless. It's easy enough to acknowledge that we use elementary math on a daily basis, slightly more involved math with accounting of any kind, a similar cousin of math in statistics in data analysis and experimentation, and very involved math in any application of physics in science or engineering. However, as the level of rigor increases, the applicability often gets more and more blurry.
