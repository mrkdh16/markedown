### Definition(s).
- $P_x(A) = P(A \mid X_0 = x)$ 
- $T_y = min \{ n \geq 1 \mid X_n = y \}$, the minimum amount of time it takes to get to state $X_n$.
- $P_{yy} = P_y(T_y \lt \infty)$, the probability $X_n$ returns to $y$ having started at $y$.
