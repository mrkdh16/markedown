Classical (Newtonian) mechanics explains a remarkable range of physical phenomena we encounter in the world around us. Of course, like many theories in physics, it was eventually found to have flaws which were later addressed by the advent of relativity. Nonetheless, classical mechanics still offers many fascinating insights. A deep understanding of it can provide a powerful, energy-based lens through which to view the world.
